How Recommendations Work

NextTrack uses content-based filtering combined with external data enhancements. Each track has audio features (energy, valence, danceability, acousticness, tempo) forming a 5-dimensional vector. We compute the centroid of your playlist to represent your taste.

External data actively boosts recommendations:

  • Influence Chains - Artists who influenced your playlist artists get a 15% boost (via Wikidata P737)
  • Similar Artists - Collaboratively-filtered similar artists get a 20% boost (via Last.fm)
  • Tag Matching - Genre/mood tag matches provide an 8% boost

Artist metadata from MusicBrainz (country, type, decade) enables filtering. Your feedback (likes/dislikes) teaches categorical preferences for future recommendations.

No account required - playlist stored in browser session (7 days). All external data cached in Redis (24-hour TTL) - no database storage.
